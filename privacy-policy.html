Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

PillPopper Daily ("the App") is developed by Roger Segura. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. The short version: your data stays on your device and we do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Information We Collect

PillPopper Daily does not collect any personal information. All medication data, schedules, caregiver details, and adherence history you enter into the App are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. If you enable iCloud sync through your device settings, your data may be synced across your Apple devices via your personal iCloud account. This sync is managed entirely by Apple and governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Camera and Photo Library

The App may request access to your camera and photo library to enable the medication label scanner feature. Images captured or selected are processed on-device using Apple's Vision framework for text recognition. No images are transmitted to external servers.

HealthKit

If you choose to enable HealthKit integration, the App may read from and write medication data to Apple Health. This data exchange happens locally on your device and is governed by Apple's HealthKit privacy protections. The App never accesses HealthKit data without your explicit permission.

Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you to take your medications. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

PillPopper Daily does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not contain any ads.

Family Sharing and Reports

The App allows you to generate medication reports and share them with caregivers. Sharing is initiated only by you and uses the standard iOS share sheet. The App does not automatically send any information to caregivers or third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete individual medications, clear your history, or remove all data by deleting the App from your device.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
