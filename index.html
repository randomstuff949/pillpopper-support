Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a medication?

Tap the "+" button on the Medications tab. You can type the medication name (with autocomplete suggestions) or use the camera button to scan a pill bottle label.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled for PillPopper Daily in Settings > Notifications. Also check that Do Not Disturb or a Focus mode is not blocking alerts.

How does the drug interaction checker work?

PillPopper Daily checks your active medications against a built-in database of known interactions. You'll see a warning when adding a medication that may interact with one you're already taking. This is informational only — always consult your doctor or pharmacist.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your medication data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

How do I share reports with my caregiver?

Go to Settings > Family Sharing, add a caregiver, then tap "Share Report." You can send it via Messages, Email, AirDrop, or any other sharing method.

Can I use Siri with PillPopper Daily?

Yes! You can set up Siri Shortcuts to log medications with your voice. Go to Settings > Siri Shortcuts to configure them.
